About KLC DevOrg

Structured community development through accountable governance

Our Story

KLC DevOrg was established to provide a structured and accountable platform for community-driven development. Recognising the need for organised leadership and sustainable asset creation, the organisation was formed as a Company Limited by Guarantee to ensure institutional stability and continuity.

KLC DevOrg operates under a governance framework that promotes transparency, accountability, and collective responsibility. Our approach focuses on building real community assets that serve present and future generations.

Legal Status & Structure

KLC DevOrg is formally incorporated as a Company Limited by Guarantee in Uganda.

This legal framework ensures:

  • Organisational continuity
  • Transparent governance
  • Structured decision-making
  • Financial accountability

Our Departments

60 structured departments grouped into functional clusters for effective coordination

KLC DevOrg operates through 60 structured departments grouped into functional clusters. These departments ensure decentralised coordination, structured leadership, effective communication, and accountability across sectors.

Administration & Legal

Departmental Leadership
